Past Events (2024 – 2025)

🌳

Inaugural Tree Planting Day

March 2024  |  Yombo Village

TJYG's first major community event. Members and local volunteers came together to plant over 200 trees along degraded hillside sections, marking the group's formal commitment to environmental conservation in Kathangachini.

✅ 200+ trees planted
🗑️

Village Clean-Up Campaign

June 2024  |  Kathangachini Sub-Location

A community-wide clean-up targeting roadsides, public spaces, and the local market area. The group collected and disposed of waste responsibly, and also distributed reusable bags to encourage lasting change in plastic use.

✅ 3 tonnes of waste collected
📋

Community Needs Assessment Forum

September 2024  |  Gatue Division

TJYG facilitated an open community forum bringing together elders, farmers, youth, and local leaders to identify the most pressing environmental and social challenges in the area, which shaped the group's 2025 work plan.

✅ 80+ community members engaged
💧

Water Harvesting Demonstration

February 2025  |  Yombo Village

A practical demonstration on building low-cost rainwater harvesting structures for home gardens. Participating families saw immediate improvements in their water access during the dry season.

✅ 15 households trained
🌿

World Environment Day Celebration

June 5, 2025  |  Kathangachini Primary School

TJYG marked World Environment Day with school children through tree planting, environmental quizzes, and a short drama on conservation. The event was covered by a local community radio station.

✅ 150+ students participated
