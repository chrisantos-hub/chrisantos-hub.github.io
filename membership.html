Join Tuinue Jamii

Be part of a movement of young people building a healthier, greener future for Kathangachini and Tharaka North.

Why Become a Member?

Membership connects you to a community that's actively making a difference.

🤝

Community Network

Connect with like-minded youth from across Kathangachini and Tharaka North working toward shared goals.

📚

Skills & Training

Gain practical skills through workshops on environmental conservation, sustainable farming, and community leadership.

🌍

Real Impact

Participate in tangible projects — tree planting, clean-ups, and health drives — that directly benefit your community.

🗣️

A Voice in Decisions

Members attend general meetings and vote on the group's direction, priorities, and use of resources.

🏅

Recognition

Outstanding contributions are recognized through certificates, appreciation letters, and spotlights in group communications.

🔗

Partnerships & Opportunities

Access opportunities from partner organizations — scholarships, internships, and community funding — as they arise.

Individual Membership

Standard Member

Open to all youth aged 18–35 in Tharaka North Sub-County

Ksh. 200 / year
  • ✔ Full participation in all TJYG events, clean-ups, and tree planting drives
  • ✔ Access to skills training workshops (farming, conservation, leadership)
  • ✔ Voting rights at Annual General Meetings and special meetings
  • ✔ Eligibility to hold an elected office in the group
  • ✔ Access to group communications: newsletters, reports, and event updates
  • ✔ Referral and linkage to partner organizations, NGOs, and government programmes
  • ✔ Certificate of membership issued annually
  • ✔ Group support during personal or family hardship (mutual aid where applicable)

How to Join

Joining TJYG is simple — follow these four steps.

1

Contact Us

Reach out via WhatsApp, phone, or email to express your interest in joining TJYG.

2

Fill a Form

Complete a short membership registration form with your personal details and contact information.

3

Pay Annual Fee

Pay the Ksh. 200 annual membership fee via M-Pesa or in person at a group meeting.

4

Welcome Aboard!

Receive your membership certificate and join our WhatsApp group to stay connected and updated.
