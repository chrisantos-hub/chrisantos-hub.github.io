About Us

Young People Building a Better Community

Tuinue Jamii Youth Group (TJYG) was founded in 2023 by a group of passionate young people from Yombo Village in Kathangachini, Tharaka North Sub-County. We believe that youth are not just the leaders of tomorrow — we are the changemakers of today.

Our work focuses on environmental conservation, sustainable agriculture, health awareness, and reducing poverty in our community. We organize tree planting drives, clean-up campaigns, educational forums, and practical training workshops.

Read Our Full Story
🌳

Environmental Conservation

Protecting forests, water catchments, and green spaces in and around Kathangachini.

🌾

Sustainable Agriculture

Training farmers in drought-resistant crops, composting, and water harvesting techniques.

🏥

Community Health

Raising awareness on health issues and linking community members to health resources.

🤝

Poverty Reduction

Empowering households through skills training and connection to livelihood opportunities.
