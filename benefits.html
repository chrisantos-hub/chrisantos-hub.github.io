Members benefit

FIRST OPPORTUNITY TO PARTICIPATE

Members are alays given first priority to participate in the events and programmes. This ensures that the members are given the best possible chance to meet the needs of the group and to contribute to the success of the organization.

UNITY

Unity makes it easier to negotiate for better prices for inputs and other resources like capacity building and other technical advisory services.

PRIDE OF RECOGNITION

Pride of recognition - joining the federation is a way to strengthen the federation so that farmers can also find satisfaction in belonging to an organization that caters to their collective interests.

STRENGTHEN THE MEMBERS' VOICE

Strengthen the members' voice is a way to build a stronger and more active group of members by encouraging them to voice their opinions and the community's needs.
