About Us

Tuinue Jamii Youth Group

A community of young people from Kathangachini working together for a cleaner, healthier, and more prosperous Tharaka North.

🎯

Our Mission

To mobilize communities, organizations, and individuals to share and participate in all aspects of environmental conservation, education, health, and poverty reduction in the Kathangachini region and beyond.

🌟

Our Vision

To be the leading youth association empowering and uplifting living standards in Kathangachini and its catchment area — building a community where every young person has the opportunity and support to thrive.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every decision we make and every project we run is guided by these five principles.

⚖️

Integrity

We are honest, transparent, and accountable in all our actions — to our members, partners, and community.

🤲

Human Dignity

We respect and uphold the worth and dignity of every person, regardless of background or circumstance.

⚡

Equality

We believe in fair and equal participation for all members, creating space for every voice to be heard.

🤝

Participation

We are stronger together. Community involvement is at the heart of everything we plan and deliver.

🔥

Dedication

We are committed to seeing our projects through — showing up consistently for our community and environment.

What We Do

Our Focus Areas

TJYG works across four interconnected areas to create lasting positive change in Kathangachini.

🌳

Environmental Conservation

Tree planting, river clean-ups, and protecting water catchment areas across Tharaka North Sub-County.

🌾

Sustainable Agriculture

Training farmers in composting, rainwater harvesting, and drought-resistant crop varieties suited to our region.

🏥

Community Health

Raising health awareness and linking community members to health services and programmes in Tharaka North.

📉

Poverty Reduction

Empowering households through skills training, financial literacy, and linkage to livelihood opportunities.
